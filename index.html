Steam Summer Sale - 2024

Today, I wondered where you’ve gone,
The paths we walked, now overgrown.
The games we played, where laughter rang,
Are now just echoes, a distant pang.

I still play those games, but not the same,
With different hands, they’ve lost their flame.
I bought a game meant for two,
Hoping one day, I'd find someone new.

But the truth I can't deny or mend,
It was you I loved, though you called me a friend.
I miss your smile, though it’s not enough,
To bring you back, or ease the rough.

How did we break what once seemed whole?
This lingering ache, it scars the soul.
The letters I wrote, now lost in the drain,
Words of love, swallowed by pain.

I tore them up, flushed them away,
Yet the feelings remain, refusing to stray.
My heart remembers, though time has passed,
No anger left, just love that lasts.

Your face, it lingers, it won’t erase,
But the hurt has faded, leaving only grace.
I miss you still, in the quiet of night,
But the weight is lighter, the burden slight.

All I hold now are memories sweet,
Of the times we shared, now incomplete.
Yet here I stand, with a heart set free,
Playing games, waiting for someone who'll see.

For love, it’s gentle, though it sometimes burns,
And I know one day, it will return.

Passenger seat - 2021

But here she was again
Listening to the same old songs over
And over again
Sometimes she comes back to her senses
To her old sayings
To her old poems, essays, words and
The most importantly
Old memories

And then she remembers only the good parts
Good for her
But she knows the bad parts though existed
How can you be so sure? asked the driver
Traces, she said
They are all over my heart
Permanent marks that will never leave
Due to the bad assembly that was made
Imagine trying to fix a bell jar while it cuts through your flesh
After each incident that tore away her heart into pieces
Indeed it had to be reassembled
But the assembly that was done once before wouldn’t be called so
Good either, but it did get the job done
Maybe that’s why it kept breaking
It was her fault anyways
Why would you have a glass heart
That could be shattered into a million pieces?

Throughout the years she made a fool of herself
Maybe people weren’t clever enough to understand this fact either
But
Apparently her heart was a huge black hole
Make-believe that lasted through her high school years
She felt as cool as “A refrigerator that has to be cooled”
Isn’t it funny, making up a lie
Just to protect yourself from the cruelty of people, of their empty hearts
Really empty hearts
Type of hearts that just pump blood

So she told herself
What was the point of hiding my heart away
Decided to take a leap of faith
She still wonders to this day
If falling taught her anything
Taking risks, and giving her heart away to a little spoiled boy
The little one thought my heart was a magic 8 ball
That he got for Christmas
Shook it around a couple of times, got some answers, got curious
How did it work
Couldn’t figure out the physics behind it
Then got bored
Didn’t ask his parents
Didn’t try to understand,
And made zero efforts
He had a bunch of other toys anyway
Why try to figure out a ball that gives you random answers?
He tossed it aside
The ball rolled over
Stopped under the bed
And there it was
Made friends with the dust and the old Hot Wheels car
Waiting to be sold to another family

Could I blame the boy? she asked
The story intrigued the driver
He responded:
Depends if he ever found you back
She told him
You don’t get people, do you?
Little spoiled kids rest little spoiled kids unless
Their house got burned down
It’s a funny way to stop being spoiled
And I like to burn houses down
Then they both looked in the rear mirror
To see the flames
But no
To answer you
The little spoiled boy never came back
He continued to swap toys
But the fundamental question is this
How would you make this boy interested in this ball

The Goat and the Watch

Have you heard the story of the sheep with a clock, or was it a goat?
Well, the story goes like this (as far as I remember):
The goat carried a heavy gold watch on a chain around its neck, and it tired him all the time.
Not only was the watch a burden, but it also didn't work.
One day, the rabbit, a friend of the goat, asked him why he carries this old heavy watch that isn't working anymore.
It looks heavy, said the rabbit.
The goat goes, yeah, you are right, it's heavy, but I've grown used to it.
To its weight, and the fact that the hand doesn't move and always shows the same thing.
One afternoon on the goat's birthday, the rabbit hands him a new watch that's glittering, really lightweight, and in perfect condition.
The goat happily hangs the watch from his neck and runs around to show it to his friends.
'You are the goat, I'm the rabbit, and the watch is your heart,' said the doctor.
'I tricked you, didn't I?' .
I love my solitude, but I'm starting to see life is too big to fit one life into it.
Hopefully, my life won't end in the near future.
Hopefully, you will be happy with your own philosophy, but most importantly, I wish you love in any form or structure you want.

Letters Never Sent

So I write to him again, the letters I will never send.
I miss him, or maybe I just miss loving him.
Loving someone, for the first and only time...
Now all alone again, naturally.
Sometimes I just wanna call him and tell him all, all about whats going on, how sad I am, how much I miss him, without waiting anything in return. I guess my anger has disappeared and all i have left for him is the love that was overshadow by my anger. Each time i listen to love songs, it reminds me of him, and his pretty voice and touch. I feel like a monster, that doesnt deserve anything… even though i did nothing wrong but love him too much. As sick as it sounds I loved him more. Someone should hold me down before I tell him everything.
